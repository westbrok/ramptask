Instructions

On some trials, a question will appear at some point while the progress bar fills up, temporarily pausing the trial.

This question will offer you the choice to gamble on the trial. If you decide to gamble, press 'g'. You will have one second to decide.

If you would rather not gamble, don't press anything and the gamble option will disappear once one second has gone by. The trial will then proceed normally.


If you decide to gamble, the trial will be stopped. However, choosing to gamble won't impact the total time a trial lasts. You will still wait the amount of time you would have if you had not decided to gamble before the next trial starts. . YOU WILL NOT FIND OUT WHETHER YOU HAVE WON OR LOST UNTIL THE END OF THE EXPERIMENT.

At the end of the experiment, one of your trials will be chosen. If you decided not to gamble on that particular trial and pressed spacebar at the correct time, the amount of money you got on that trial will be added as a bonus to your earnings.

If you DID decide to gamble on that particular trial, a roulette wheel will appear and the gamble will be initated. If you gambled and won, the amount of money will be added as a bonus to your earnings.

The first three trials will be clearly marked practice trials that won't be picked for the bonus trial.
